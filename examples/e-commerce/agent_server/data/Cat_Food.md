Feeding Instructions
A single morning serving will allow your cat to nibble freely throughout the day. The serving size should be adjusted based on your cat's age and activity level. When introducing Meow Mix Original Choice cat food, gradually mix it with your cat's current food over the course of 7-10 days. Always provide your cat with a bowl of fresh water.
